About these platforms

Each platform is an educational reference implementation that models a modern bank as a landscape of BIAN-style service domains — independently owned, built, tested and deployed. Every constituent service has its own repository and its own live documentation site. They are learning artifacts, not production banking software, and are not affiliated with or certified by BIAN, OBIE / Open Banking Limited, or any Shariah authority.
